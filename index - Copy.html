Features

A little bit about AmityCoin

Zero Premine

We believe a premine is not in the spirit of Crypto. We are here for the tech not a payday.

Cryptonote Variant Softshell

AmityCoin uses the Proof of Work Crypotonote Variant, this makes AmityCoin ASIC, GPU, FPGA and Pool Resistant. The only way to mine AmityCoin is to Solo Mine with a CPU. Due to the adaptive nature of Soft Shell, creating pool software becomes impractical

Reward unlock time

AmityCoin implements the LWMA-3 difficuty algorithm and a unique block reward unlock time of 12 hours.
